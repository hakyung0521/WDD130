Overview

Purpose

The purpose of my site is to tell people the recipes of foods by country and to know the recipes of your favorite chefs. It takes a long time for people to buy many books or compare many sites through search each time to find the recipe of the dish they want to make. Therefore, this website helps them use their time efficiently to find the recipe for the dish they want and helps them try various dishes by country. In addition, this site can generate income by advertising products related to cooking and receiving subscription fees from users.

Audience

The type of people who want to visit this site are people who love to cook and want to find recipes for their favorite dishes. When building this site, we keep in mind people who want to challenge themselves to create new and different foods. The content of this site will appear as a variety of cooking recipes, the colors will make the dishes look more delicious with warm colors, and the recipes will be easy to read through clean fonts. Also, my audience typically accesses my site through desktops, laptops, and mobile devices.

Content

Home page

The recipe homepage serves as an introduction to the site's main sections, which include World Food Recipes and Cooking or Healthy Food Tips. Visitors can navigate through these sections using the main navigation menu. The homepage aims to provide visitors with a clear overview of the site's content and encourage them to explore and discover new recipes and cooking skills.

Images for the Home page

Recipes

This page will feature a collection of recipes from different countries and cultures. Visitors can browse through various categories and explore traditional dishes from around the world. The page will showcase the diversity of flavors and ingredients and provide step-by-step guides on how to prepare these dishes.

Images for the recipes page

Tips

This page will provide visitors with helpful resources on cooking techniques, kitchen tips, and healthy eating tips. The page will feature articles on how to improve your cooking skills, basic cooking techniques, and how to choose and use different kitchen tools. Visitors can also share their cooking tips and tricks with others.
