Overview

Purpose

The purpose of my site is to tell people the recipes of foods by country and to know the recipes of your favorite chefs. It takes a long time for people to buy many books or compare many sites through search each time to find the recipe of the dish they want to make. Therefore, this website helps them use their time efficiently to find the recipe for the dish they want and helps them try various dishes by country. In addition, this site can generate income by advertising products related to cooking and receiving subscription fees from users.

Audience

The type of people who want to visit this site are people who love to cook and want to find recipes for their favorite dishes. When building this site, we keep in mind people who want to challenge themselves to create new and different foods. The content of this site will appear as a variety of cooking recipes, the colors will make the dishes look more delicious with warm colors, and the recipes will be easy to read through clean fonts. Also, my audience typically accesses my site through desktops, laptops, and mobile devices.
